Research

Overview

My research interests are organized around one question: how can we design computational systems that are accurate, inspectable, and useful under real constraints?

Themes

Reliable Learning Systems

  • evaluation methods for model behavior under distribution shift;

  • uncertainty estimates and calibration;

  • debugging workflows for learned systems.

Optimization and Numerical Methods

  • first-order methods for large-scale optimization;

  • practical tradeoffs between accuracy, stability, and runtime;

  • mathematical tooling for reproducible research notes.

Research Infrastructure

  • static research websites, literate notes, and lightweight publishing;

  • experiment tracking that remains useful after a project ends;

  • small tools that reduce friction in reading, writing, and reviewing.
