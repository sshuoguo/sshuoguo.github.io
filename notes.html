  • Acceleration and Stability Regions of First-Order Methods on Strongly Convex Quadratics

    My thoughts: This paper studies the tradeoff between acceleration and stability for first-order methods on strongly convex quadratic problems. What I find especially interesting is that acceleration is not simply “faster is better” — it often comes with a smaller stability region, which means the method becomes more fragile to step-size choices and perturbations.
